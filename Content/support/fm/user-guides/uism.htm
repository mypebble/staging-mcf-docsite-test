Universal Infant Free School Meals

In order to record and report on Universal Infant Free School Meals, you will need access to Fund Manager’s Register tab. If you haven’t got it, please get in touch with Pebble’s Sales Team.

Highlights

From September 2014, all pupils in reception, year 1 and year 2 in state-funded schools in England will be eligible for free school meals (UIFSMs).

To help you manage this, you can assign pupils to a UIFSM group within Fund Manager and generate reports to keep track of the number of free meals that are being taken.

Creating a UIFSM Group and Adding Eligible Pupils

In order to record the number of Universal Infant Free School Meals that are being taken, you will need to create a UIFSM group to which you can assign your eligible pupils.

Note: For the Universal Infant Free School Meals reports to function correctly, all pupils must be assigned to a Group called Pupils. You can do this via the Manage Names screen.

Go to Administration > Manage Names and click Create Group in the Group Actions section.

In the Manage Groups box, Enter a Name for your Universal Infant Free School Meals group and select “User Defined” as the Group Type.

Once you have created your Universal Infant Free School Meals group you will need to add your eligible pupils.

You can do this by clicking the checkbox next to each pupil in Manage Names, selecting the group via Group Actions then clicking Add to Group

Assigning a UIFSM Group to your Register Account

When you have created your Universal Infant Free School Meals group and added your eligible pupils, you will need to assign it to your Register Account in order to record the number of free meals being taken.

Go to Administration > Manage Accounts and click on the account name for your Register Account. This will then bring up the Update Account screen.

In the UIFSM Group box, click on the arrow on the right and select the Universal Infant Free School Meals group you have created.

If you have pupils who are eligible for Free School Meals, these take precedence over Universal Infant Free School Meals. If this is the case, select your Free School Meals group in the Exclusion Group box.

Recording UIFSM Taken Items

To manually record the number of Universal Infant Free School Meals that have been taken, go to Register > Record Taken Items and select the Account, Group, Class and Week Commencing Date that you want to record the items for then click Search.

This will bring back a list of all pupils within the Group and Class you have defined. Click the checkbox next to each pupil for the days you want to record taken items for.

Reporting on UIFSM Taken Items

There are a number of ways to report on the number of Universal Infant Free School Meals that have been taken.

In Register > Taken Items you can see the total number of Taken Items for an Account, Group and / or Class.

For pupils assigned to your Universal Infant Free School Meals group, taken items will be automatically recorded as “U” (UIFSM); for pupils assigned to your Free School Meals group, taken items will be recorded as “F” (Free); for pupils assigned to neither group, taken items will be recorded as “P” (Paid).

To see more detail on pupils taking Universal Infant Free School Meals, go to Register > Names Summary.

The Receipts, Payments, Balance and Final Balance columns are the sum the amount (£) for each pupil; the Paid, Free and UIFSM columns are a count of the number of meals taken.

The Free column next to Class indicates whether the pupil is eligible for Free School Meals-related free school meals.

To see the taken items statement for an individual pupil, go to Register > Statement and select the Contact, the Account, the Date From the Date To then click Search.

For a complete report on the total meals taken, the total number of free meals and the total number of Universal Free School Meals go to Register > Universal Free School Meals and select the Account and Date From/To for the report.
